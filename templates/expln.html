How to format the ".txt" file to include all of the question's details

{
%N Q (The number should be separated by space or a character from the question)
%O o (max of 4)
answer=A
}

where N is number of question
where O is option identifier (max of 4, must be alphabet)
where answer= is answer identifier
where Q is question
where o is option
where A is the answer


example
%1 A small handcart, one wheel and tire shafts used for moving farm crop material is ?
%a cup %b wheelbarrow %c kettle
answer=wheelbarrow
. . .
%14 Weeds are ?
%a planted plants %b timber tree %c unwanted plants
answer=unwanted plants
. . .
